FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

Everything you need to know before you book your trip.

What's included in the package?
All packages include accommodation, meals as specified, transport, sightseeing tours, and a professional guide. Luxury packages include additional amenities like private vehicles and premium experiences.
What is the cancellation policy?
You can cancel up to 14 days before the trip for a full refund. Cancellations within 7 days are subject to a 50% charge. Please refer to our detailed policy for more information.
What is the best time to visit Kashmir?
Kashmir is beautiful year-round. Spring (March–May) is great for gardens, summer (June–August) for pleasant weather, autumn (September–November) for foliage, and winter (December–February) for snow sports.
Are the tours family-friendly?
Absolutely! We offer family-friendly packages with activities suitable for all ages, including gentle sightseeing, cultural experiences, and comfortable accommodation.
Is transport included?
Yes, all packages include comfortable transport. Starter packages include shared transport, while Luxury and Ultimate packages include private vehicles with dedicated drivers.
Do I need a passport or visa?
International travelers need a valid passport and Indian tourist visa. Our team can assist with documentation guidance. Domestic travelers require valid government ID.
