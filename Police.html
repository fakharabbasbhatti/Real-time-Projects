HISTORICAL MILESTONES

Law Enforcement Timeline

1947
Pakistan Police Established
The Pakistan Police Service was formally constituted at independence to maintain law and order across the new nation.
1974
FIA Founded
Federal Investigation Agency established to combat federal crimes, immigration offenses, and banking fraud at the national level.
1997
Motorway Police Launched
With the inauguration of the M-2 Lahore–Islamabad Motorway, a dedicated Motorway Police force was established.
2004
Police Order 2002 Implemented
Major reform legislation transformed policing structure, establishing district public safety commissions and accountability mechanisms.
2018
FIA Cyber Crime Wing Strengthened
Prevention of Electronic Crimes Act enabled FIA to expand cyber crime enforcement capabilities significantly.
2024
NEXUS SHIELD Digital Command System
Launch of Pakistan's first integrated digital law enforcement command platform, unifying all agencies under one network.
