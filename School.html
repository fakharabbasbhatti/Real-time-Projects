Roadmap

The Student Journey

Step 01

Online Application

Complete the digital admission form, upload required documents, and submit for review. Receive instant acknowledgement with your application ID.

Step 02

Assessment & Interview

Attend the entrance assessment and parent-teacher interview. Results are communicated within 48 hours.

Step 03

Enrolment & ID Card

Finalise fee payment, select subjects, and collect your smart student ID card with RFID access.

Step 04

Smart Classes Begin

Access AI-powered interactive classrooms, digital textbooks, and the student portal from Day 1.

Step 05

Continuous Assessment

Regular quizzes, projects, and assignments with instant AI-driven feedback to keep performance on track.

Step 06

Examinations & Graduation

Sit secure online board exams, receive certified results, and graduate with distinction—ready for the next chapter.
